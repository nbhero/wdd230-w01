W01 Milestone - The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

California Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

It will serve as a central hub for businesses within California. The main focus is to promote local businesses, foster economic growth, provide resources and support entrepreneurs, facilitate networking opportunities, and advocate for the interests of the business community.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming networking events or workshops are being organized by the Chamber?
  • What are the benefits of becoming a member of the Chamber of Commerce?
  • Are there resources available to help me with business planning or funding opportunities?
